Importance of learning DevOps engineer in software development cycle
Learning about DevOps engineering will be very usefull in the software development world. Some things you will gain from learning DevOps engineering is, Faster delivery: DevOps aims to automate processes wherever possible, this leads to faster delivery of software updates and new features. Improved Quality: Through automation, testing, and monitoring, DevOps helps identify and address issues early in the development process, this results in higher software quality. Risk Management: By implementing automated testing, monitoring and deployment practices, DevOps reduces the risk associated with software releases and updates.
